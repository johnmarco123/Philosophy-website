Top 10 Books To Make You A Better Person

1.) The Obstacle Is The Way

Book in a sentence

This outstanding book reveals with stories, quotes, and logic that every obstacle is an opportunity to improve yourself as a person.

Main points

  • The three main areas to change to overcome any obstacle are perception, action, and will.
  • Obstacles are opportunities to practice virtues.
  • No matter how small the task, do it with the best of your ability.

Most Impactful Quotes

The obstacle in the path becomes the path. Never forget, within every obstacle is an opportunity to improve out condition.

"The goal is:
Not: I'm okay with this.
Not: I think I feel good about this.
But: I feel great about it.
Because if it happened, then it was meant to happen, and I am glad that it did when it did. I am meant to make the best of it."

Book summary

This is one of the most outstanding books there is on the market for dealing with life's many challenges. The book is based upon Stoicism, the ancient Greek philosophy of enduring pain or adversity with perseverance and resilience. Its primary focus is on three core areas, perception, action and will. The book explains that when you face an obstacle, if you view it as something that's unfortunate and painful, it will be exactly that. As it has been written in the bible ‚ÄúAsk, and it will be given to you; seek, and you will find; knock, and it will be opened to you.‚Äù (Matthew 7:7) and this book proves this very point. It shows you that obstacles are teachers that often teach you what you aren't currently looking to learn. The book also highlights that most obstacles that are challenging usually are excellent opportunities to practice your virtues.
